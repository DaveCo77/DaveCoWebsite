Technical Specifications

Engineered for Live Production

Physical distance shouldn't break your show flow. Keep your entire crew synced with a digital matrix that runs flawlessly on any web-enabled device.

TECH 01

Device Agnostic Endpoints

Works flawlessly on iPads, laptops, or mobile phones. Generate a unique URL in the Master Control, copy it, and load it on any device to instantly designate it as a secure comms terminal.

TECH 02

Global Show Cues

The Master Control panel features one-click "Standby" (Amber) and "Show Go" (Green) global signals. Triggering a cue instantly forces all connected terminals to flash the corresponding color.

TECH 03

Stream Moderation Tools

Built with Twitch-style moderation logic. Lock global input to instantly freeze chatter during a critical cue, and pin priority announcements to the absolute top of every endpoint's screen.

TECH 04

Atmospheric Modifiers

Need to subtly grab the crew's attention without sending a message? Trigger the "Breathe Red" modifier to cast a slow, pulsing vignette across the entire site's terminal screens.
