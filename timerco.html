Technical Specifications

Engineered for Live Production

Everything you need to keep your show running on time and keep your presenters perfectly informed without the lag.

SPEC 01

Zero-Latency Math Engine

Instead of relying on network pings every second, TimerCo calculates the clock locally on the stage monitor. If the venue Wi-Fi drops out, the timer keeps ticking flawlessly.

SPEC 02

Flash Messaging

Need to tell a speaker to skip the next slide? Type a custom message at the FOH control desk and flash it instantly to the screen in a high-visibility yellow alert.

SPEC 03

Overtime Alerts

When the clock hits zero, it automatically begins counting upwards into the negative, and the entire DSM screen smoothly pulses red to firmly let the presenter know they need to wrap up.

SPEC 04

One-Click Deployment

No software to install on the venue's laptops. Simply hit 'Copy DSM Link' and drop it into a browser on any iPad or monitor to instantly link it to your control desk.
