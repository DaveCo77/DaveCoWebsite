Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction

At Pixel Array, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our seating chart software ("TableCo").

2. Data We Collect

We collect information to provide our services and improve your experience:

  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.
  • Event Data: The seating chart configurations, guest names, and table arrangements you input into the software.
  • Usage Data: Analytics provided by Google Analytics to help us understand how users interact with our website.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to facilitate your event planning. This includes providing the kiosk and control panel functionality and communicating with you regarding your account or support requests.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to run TableCo:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing.
  • Firebase (Google): For hosting our database and ensuring real-time syncing.
  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. While no method of digital storage is 100% secure, we strive to protect your event data as we would our own.
